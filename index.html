Hi! I'm a 3rd year PhD student in Computer Science at Columbia University. I am super fortunate to be advised by Professor Steven Feiner! I'm also a part of the Computer Graphics and User Interfaces (CGUI) Lab as well as the Columbia HCI group.

As an M.S. student, I also worked under Professor Junfeng Yang. Before Columbia, I received a B.S. in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Cognitive Science from Rutgers University.

Research

My research focuses on proxies and digital twins for interaction techniques in XR. I am also interested in the intersection of XR and AI. My recent works explore:

  • Teleoperation and Telepresence (ISMAR'25, IEEEVR'25)
  • Rock Climbing and XR (ISMAR'24)
  • XR for Software Engineering (ISMAR'24)
